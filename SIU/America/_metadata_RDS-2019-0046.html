Description:
Abstract:
These data document the summary of species, by taxonomic group, listed as threatened or endangered by the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service from 1 July 1976 to 30 September 2019 for the United States. Data were compiled from two sources: the Endangered Species Bulletins and the Environmental Conservation Online system (ECOS), http://ecos.fws.gov/tess_public/reports/box-score-report.
Purpose:
To create a temporal data set of species listed as threatened or endangered, by taxonomic group. The data are provided because the Fish & Wildlife Service does not provide temporal counts of threatened and endangered species, only counts of single points in time.

Data collected are counts of threatened and endangered species in the United States.

These counts:

(a) include populations in which the United States shares jurisdiction with another nation;

(b) include 22 animal species (14 in the U.S.3 and 8 Foreign) that are counted more than once, primarily because these animals have distinct population segments (each with its own individual listing status);

(c ) include the following groups:
Plants,
Animals,
Amphibians,

Birds,

Corals,
Crustaceans,
Fishes,
Insects,
Mammals,
Reptiles,
Snails, and
Mollusks (Snails and Clams combined);

(d) do not include listings for species with a status of "Similarity of Appearance."

Process_Step:
Process_Description:
Data were compiled in periodic intervals for the number of species listed and recovery plans, by group, from the Endangered Species Bulletin publications and Environmental Conservation Online System (ECOS) Box Score. Groups reported were amphibians, arachnids, birds, clams, corals, crustaceans, fishes, insects, mammals, reptiles, snails, total plants, and grand totals.

Data were manually transcribed from publication or online report into a SAS software program that created the master data set.

Overview_Description:
Entity_and_Attribute_Overview:
Below you will find a complete list and description of the files available in this data publication.

NOTE: data files are available in two different file formats (CSV and XLSX)

\Data\danger.csv: Comma-delimited ASCII text file containing the summary of U.S. threatened and endangered species from 1 July 1976 through 30 September 2019, by taxonomic group. Data compiled from the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service's Endangered Species Bulletin and Environmental Conservation Online System.

\Data\danger_modified_mollusk.csv: Comma-delimited ASCII text file containing the summary of U.S. threatened and endangered species from 1 July 1976 through 30 September 2019, by taxonomic group. Four time periods (19 March 2008, 13 May 2008, 13 August 2008, and 28 November 2008) were modified due to an accounting change tied to species collectively referenced as O'ahu tree snails (Achatinella spp.). Data compiled from the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service's Endangered Species Bulletin and Environmental Conservation Online System.

\Data\variable_descriptions.csv: Comma-delimited ASCII text file containing the variable name and description for danger.csv and danger_modified_mollusk.csv.


\Data\danger.xlsx: Microsoft Excel Open XML spreadsheet file containing (a) the summary of U.S. threatened and endangered species from 1 July 1976 through 30 September, by taxonomic group, in sheet labelled "danger" and (b) the metadata defining the variables, sheet labelled "variable_descriptions". Data compiled from the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service's Endangered Species Bulletin and Environmental Conservation Online System.

\Data\danger_modified_mollusk.xlsx: Microsoft Excel Open XML spreadsheet file containing (a) the summary of U.S. threatened and endangered species from 1 July 1976 through 30 September, by taxonomic group, in sheet labelled "danger_modified_mollusk" and (b) the metadata defining the variables, sheet labelled "variable_descriptions". Four time periods (19 March 2008, 13 May 2008, 13 August 2008, and 28 November 2008) were modified due to an accounting change ties to species collectively referenced as O'ahu tree snails (Achatinella spp.). Data compiled from the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service's Endangered Species Bulletin and Environmental Conservation Online System.
