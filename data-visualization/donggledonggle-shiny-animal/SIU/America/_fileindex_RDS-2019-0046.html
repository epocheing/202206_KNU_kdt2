Summary of U.S. threatened and endangered species listings, 1976-2019

Research Data Publication File Index

File Folder Description
_metadata_RDS-2019-0046.html   Metadata file in HTML format containing a description of the content, quality, and other characteristics of the data.
_metadata_RDS-2019-0046.xml   Metadata file in Extensible Markup Language (XML) format containing a description of the content, quality, and other characteristics of the data.
danger.csv \Data Comma-delimited ASCII text file containing the summary of U.S. threatened and endangered species from 1 July 1976 through 30 September 2019, by taxonomic group. Data compiled from the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service's Endangered Species Bulletin and Environmental Conservation Online System. (Data also available as XLSX file.)
danger_modified_mollusk.csv \Data Comma-delimited ASCII text file containing the summary of U.S. threatened and endangered species from 1 July 1976 through 30 September 2019, by taxonomic group. Four time periods (19 March 2008, 13 May 2008, 13 August 2008, and 28 November 2008) were modified due to an accounting change tied to species collectively referenced as O'ahu tree snails (Achatinella spp.). Data compiled from the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service's Endangered Species Bulletin and Environmental Conservation Online System. (Data also available as XLSX file.)
variable_descriptions.csv \Data Comma-delimited ASCII text file containing the variable name and description for danger.csv and danger_modified_mollusk.csv.
danger.xlsx \Data Microsoft Excel Open XML spreadsheet file containing (a) the summary of U.S. threatened and endangered species from 1 July 1976 through 30 September, by taxonomic group, in sheet labelled "danger" and (b) the metadata defining the variables, sheet labelled "variable_descriptions". Data compiled from the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service's Endangered Species Bulletin and Environmental Conservation Online System. (Data also available as CSV file.)
danger_modified_mollusk.xlsx \Data Microsoft Excel Open XML spreadsheet file containing (a) the summary of U.S. threatened and endangered species from 1 July 1976 through 30 September, by taxonomic group, in sheet labelled "danger_modified_mollusk" and (b) the metadata defining the variables, sheet labelled "variable_descriptions". Four time periods (19 March 2008, 13 May 2008, 13 August 2008, and 28 November 2008) were modified due to an accounting change ties to species collectively referenced as O'ahu tree snails (Achatinella spp.). Data compiled from the U.S. Fish & Wildlife Service's Endangered Species Bulletin and Environmental Conservation Online System. (Data also available as CSV file.)
